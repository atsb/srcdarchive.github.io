Description: In the past week or so, I have been thinking of doing a UV-Max movie run for Ultimate Doom, basically
all 36 maps done with 100% kills and secrets. Looks like there is tons of room for improvement, but given Ultimate
Doom's more "slower" gameplay compared to say Doom 2 and the difficulty of Episode 4, not to mention making sure
you have some little things to worry about such as enemies going around places you have already been to, well dying
is something to avoid at all times. In fact the biggest priority here is to not die and do the Max requirements, and
so I did. Kind of wish I didn't spend extra time making sure all enemies have spawn in say that teleporter trap in
E3M5 or going around a map making sure no Cacodemon or Imp is on the other side of the map as I press the exit
button. There's also other quirks such as having at least one rocket to access E3M9, having enough health to prepare
for E4M6, that NIN secret in E4M1. It's easier to do just one episode compared to doing all four of them and by the
point I reached Episode 4, the last thing I want is to go back to the beginning and start over. After finishing
E4M6, from then on it's a cakewalk in E4M7 and E4M8. Episode 1 is no problem, Episode 2 is a bit tricky in some maps
like E2M5 AND E2M6, Episode 3 is short(ish) but some maps like E4M4 are not to joke around regardless, and Episode
4 is self-explanatory.

Regardless, I might get back to doing another UV-Max movie run, but that's for another time, I am kind of glad
enough to have completed a run without dying and 100% kills and secrets (when possible E1M8, E4M3, and E4M7 have
their own issues with UV-Max) on every map in the game.
